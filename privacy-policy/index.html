How do we store your Data?

We securely store your data on a secure server hosted by Versio B.V. in The Netherlands. Security precautions we take to ensure that your data is secure include appropriate access control, auditing of connections, authentication of visitors, encryption of data and credentials and regular backups.

The Requestor web application is built with security in mind, which means measures against serious risks (i.e. SQL Injection, Sensitive Data Exposure and Cross-Site Scripting) are taken. To learn more about common security risks, please visit OWASP's Top Ten Application Security Risks.

The Requestor web application will keep your project- and evaluation data for a total of six months. Once this time period has expired, we will automatically delete your data. Prior to this, you will receive reminders through e-mail. You can request to keep the data for a longer- or even for an indefinite period of time. This can be configured in project settings.

What are your Data Protection Rights?

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all your data protection rights. Every user of the Requestor web application is entitled to the following:

  1. The right to access You have the right to ask us for copies of your personal data.
  2. The right to rectification You have the right to ask that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to ask us to complete information you believe is incomplete.
  3. The right to erasure You have the right to ask us to erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  4. The right to restrict processing You have the right to ask us to restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  5. The right to data portability You have the right to ask us to transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of the rights listed above, please contact us at our email: privacy@arthurgeel.com.
