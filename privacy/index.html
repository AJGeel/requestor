What are your Data Protection Rights?

Requestor would like to make sure you are fully aware of all your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  1. The right to access You have the right to ask Requestor for copies of your personal data.
  2. The right to rectification You have the right to ask that Requestor correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to ask Requestor to complete information you believe is incomplete.
  3. The right to erasure You have the right to ask Requestor to erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  4. The right to restrict processing You have the right to ask Requestor restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  5. The right to data portability You have the right to ask Requestor transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email: privacy@arthurgeel.com.
